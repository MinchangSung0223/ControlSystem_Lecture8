$\bigstar$ Dirac delta function is mathematically defined as:
\begin{enumerate}
	\item Approximation
	\begin{align*}
		F_{\Delta t}(t) = \begin{cases}1/\Delta t & 0 < t \leq \Delta t \\0 & otherwise \end{cases}\\
		\delta(t) =  \lim_{\Delta t\rightarrow 0} F_{\Delta t}(t) 
	\end{align*}
